Return Policy

Donations and Payments: All donations made to Prajapathi Foundation are non-refundable. Please ensure you review your contribution details carefully before completing the transaction.

Merchandise Returns

  • The item must be returned within 7 days of receipt.
  • The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as delivered.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items, such as digital products, personalized items, or perishable goods, are not eligible for returns or refunds.

Return Process

  • To initiate a return, please contact us at prajapaticsf@gmail.com or 9137594818.
  • Provide proof of purchase (order number or receipt) and a detailed reason for the return.
  • If the return is approved, instructions will be provided for shipping the item back.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to an error on our part, such as receiving a defective or incorrect item.

Refunds

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7 days after receiving and inspecting the returned item.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this return policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
